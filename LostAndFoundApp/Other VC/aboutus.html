About Us




Lost&Found Application is a project work, designed and developed with the contribution of Naina Vaghasiya(C0753560)and Shivani Dhiman (C0751731)

Lost&Found sets the home ground for the lost and found items. Contemporarily, the concept deals with the idea of adding lost and found items and keeping a track over both sides as a user.

Features

  • Innumerable addition of items
  • Track the items lost and found as a list
  • Put up relevant picture in order to give detailed description of the subject.
  • Rewards by the owner
  • Easy Sign Up

Follow Up Steps

  1. Login or Signup as a User
  2. Add the lost/found item details
  3. Upload the picture, if an item is lost, while for found ads, hints will be dropped.
  4. Categorize the type of item lost
  5. Track the status of the lost or the found item.
